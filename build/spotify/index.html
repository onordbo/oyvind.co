Spotify Match

Discover music that you'll love. Together.

UI Design

Project:

Spotify Match is an interactive campaign concept, based on a simple insight: People love discussing music. We took this insight and turned it into the campaign: «Spotify Match – Discover music that you’ll love. Together.»

Spotify Match is an interactive social campaign, that aims to strengthen Spotify as a platform for creating new connections with people that share the same love for music as Spotify does.

We wanted to grow the Spotify brand to more than just delivering music to the entire world. We wanted people to believe that Spotify can be a true hub for the social music community. A place where people can connect with one another.

Role & Impact:

Conceptualization, User Interface Design

I lead the initial brainstorming team, and ran a design team of three individuals. Over the course of three weeks, we took the concept from a fluffy idea, to a ready to go concept with high-fidelity mockups to illustrate our ideas.

I specifically, designed the mockups of the user-interface that we presented to the user upon landing on our campaign site. Furthermore I built iterations together with my team for the remainders of the visual pieces for the campaign.
