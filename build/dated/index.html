Dated

Your stuff expires, you know.

Project:

App Concept, User Interface Design

This is a very simple project. I recently got tired of finding expired milk cartons in my fridge, so I decided to do something about it. What you see before you is the simplest reminder app you will ever use.

Because you’re probably too busy thinking about that really special project you’re currently working on, to remember that your food is molding in your fridge.

Dated requires exactly five user interactions. No more, no less. You give your item a name and an expiry date. Submit. That’s it.

You can change the notification sound if you feel like it. Even the expiry date reminder from the default «two days before», if you’re feeling funky.

Give me more.
