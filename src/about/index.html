My name is Øyvind Nordbø, and I’m a Product Designer from Norway. Or a developer. Or Project Manager. You know what? You can call me whatever you want. Call me a slob if you feel like it. I don’t really care what my title is. If you really want to flatter me, call me a talented generalist.

I'm a digital hacker. I start projects with a mindset to finish it, even if it means learning something I have no idea how works. I love to design stuff in Sketch and write code in my favorite text-editor, Sublime text.

I believe in lifelong learning, where every new challenge is a new opportunity to explore something new within the industry. I don’t subscribe to specific labeling when it comes to design, because design is so fluffy that one never seems to find a coherent definition.

I love exploring new stuff. Ever since I dug up Photoshop from my fathers half-busted laptop when I was a little kid, I’ve always found technology and beauty in combination to be very fascinating.

On my free time I build stuff for the internet, and spend time staring at a half full wine glass, pretending like I know something about it. I love hackathons and design sprints. I contributed to Swipe and Drooodle, two products that are currently live and running. Born at Startup Weekend Oslo.
